Learning Path:

We offer 3 types of courses for our high school students to choose from. For each subject, there is Higher Level (HL) and Standard Level (SL)
  • Diploma Programme - 6 subjects (Min. 3 HL and 3 SL), Extended Essay (EE), Theory of Knowledge (ToK), and Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS)
  • Diploma Courses Programme - 5 subjects (Min. 1 HL) and CAS
  • High School Diploma - 3 subjects and CAS
